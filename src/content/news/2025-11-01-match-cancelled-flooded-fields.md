# Match Cancelled Due to Weather

Our scheduled home match against RMSC U11B14D3 (Johnson) at Miller Park West on Saturday, November 1st has been **cancelled due to flooded fields** caused by heavy rainfall.

## What Happened

The heavy rain made the field conditions unsafe for play, and the league made the decision to cancel the match to ensure player safety.

## What's Next

- The match may be rescheduled later in the season
- Check the schedule page for updates on the rescheduled date
- We'll use this time to continue our excellent training momentum

## Looking Ahead

While it's disappointing to miss our home match, especially after our first win, the team will stay focused and ready. We have practices scheduled this week to continue building on our success.

Stay tuned for updates on the rescheduled match date!
